THE KLASSIT STORY

Built for Learners
Built for Educators

At Klassit, our compelling story is steeped in real-world experience, innovation, and the unwavering pursuit of educational excellence. Founded by a seasoned teacher and instructor, our platform has deep roots in the world of education.


Our founder, with a wealth of experience nurturing and empowering creatives / entrepreneurs in the realms of design, strategy, branding, marketing, and creativity, embarked on a mission in 2018. This mission took shape as UU SERIES, an initiative that brought learners and experts together in dynamic in-person masterclasses.


However, in 2020, the world changed dramatically with the onset of the pandemic. UU SERIES swiftly pivoted to offer online classes with dedicated instructors, adapting to the evolving educational landscape.


Since inception of the UU SERIES, we've welcomed over 1000 learners to our UU SERIES Masterclasses, both in-person and online.

As a learner, Klassit is built with you in mind. It was spurred by the need to make finding and scheduling these beneficial one-on-one and group classes with knowledgeable instructors easy for you. We believe in the magic that happens when knowledge flows seamlessly and interaction exists between teacher and student.


As an instructor on Klassit, our commitment goes beyond facilitating connections with learners. We are aware of the particular difficulties you face as an instructor when promoting your classes. By taking on that responsibility, Klassit guarantees that instructors' knowledge is promoted to a broad audience.


Welcome to Klassit, where knowledge meets innovation and where we're committed to improving education one class at a time.


Whatever it is, just Klass it.
